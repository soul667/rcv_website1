### About Me
Ravi joins the lab from an embedded systems background and is interested in data-efficient manipulation. He prototypes robotic hands and pipelines that connect perception with grasp synthesis.

### Research
1. Learning-based grasp planning under limited supervision
2. Tactile and vision fusion for dexterous manipulation
3. Hardware-aware policy deployment on mobile manipulators
