### About Me
Lina will join the RCV Lab in Fall 2025 to focus on robust multi-sensor perception for mobile robots. She previously interned on visual-inertial mapping projects and enjoys deploying research code onto field robots.

### Research
1. Multimodal localization in dense urban campuses
2. Event and RGB fusion for long-horizon SLAM
3. Trustworthy perception for autonomous inspection platforms
